I am an engineering student at the University of Waterloo. I study Mechatronics engineering and am interested in many different disciplines. Currently, I am doing a co-op term at Gastronomous Technologies in Oakville, Ontario as a mechatronics engineer. In university, some of my favourite courses to study are related to materials, physics, and algorithms.

When I'm not working or studying, I like to spend my time designing, inventing, and exploring. Recently I’ve been working on my DIY CNC router, and hopefully, it will start helping me with future projects, allowing me to have access to additional manufacturing technologies.

Friends and family won’t hesitate to tell you that I will never stop creating new things, I have a deep passion for making and experimenting with new ideas. Whether it be designing new things to be 3D printed on my Prusa Mini or milled on my new CNC router, it always brings me joy to see something from my mind come to fruition.

Aside from engineering and projects, I love to explore new places. From travelling, camping, hiking, or even just adventuring around the city, experiencing new things is thrilling for me (I also love trying new food!).
