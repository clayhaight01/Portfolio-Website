Let al*rm clocks be a thing of the past, as there should be nothing alarming about waking up every morning. Kurt is designed to use a combination of natural light and sound, along with clever timing to give you a more elegant awakening.


Near your typical bedtime, Kurt uses you closing your curtains as a signal that you are going to sleep, and can be configured to wake you up after a given amount of time, to wake you up at the same time everyday, or slowly help move your sleep cycle by shifting your wakeup time and reminding you when to sleep.

Kurt is designed to be versatile, and works on almost every curtain style window dressing with minimal installation effort. A soft, stretchy silicone strap stretches to fit curtain rods from ⅝ - 1 ½ inches without leaving any marks.
