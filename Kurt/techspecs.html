Kurt V1 is a feature packed robotic system with sensors and actuators packaged tightly in a beautiful form factor. With dimensions just larger than a credit card, it easily hides away within the folds of a curtain.

Hardware Design

Completely custom PCB

ESP12E @ 60MHz with Wifi

Custom H bridge motor and solenoid drivers

88dB speaker with onboard amplifier

4GB of MP3 storage

Multiplexed sensor suite including two hall effect sensors and stall detection

Mechanical Design

DC brushed motor with 298:1 stage gearbox for high torque

Clutched design allows for low friction backdriving

Micro solenoid for clutch activation

Engaged clutch can be mechanically tuned for a specific slippage torque

Industrial Design

Completely screwless enclosure using dovetails and locking features

Swappable acrylic sheets for style matching (renders have semi transparent crimson red)

Machined aerospace grade aluminum accents

Silicone strap for adjustability on different sized curtain rods
